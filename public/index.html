Service access

LinkUp ride services require an approved university account, verified email, and agreement to the latest Terms of Service and Privacy Notice.

Ride safety

Riders and drivers must provide accurate trip, seat, vehicle, pickup, and drop-off information. Do not use LinkUp to create unsafe, misleading, or unauthorized rides.

Payments and reservations

Seats are only reserved after checkout is completed. Driver-set prices, payout details, and commission rules must be accurate before a ride is listed or accepted.

Location sharing

Terms of Service

These Terms are a starter template for LinkUp and should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before real users, payments, or public launch.

Service role

LinkUp is a student ride marketplace that helps users offer, request, reserve, and coordinate rides. LinkUp is not the driver, transportation carrier, insurer, or employer of drivers.

User responsibilities

Users must provide accurate information, use their own university email, obey applicable laws, respect other users, and avoid unsafe, fraudulent, harassing, or illegal conduct.

Driver responsibilities

Drivers are responsible for legal driving eligibility, safe operation, accurate ride details, vehicle condition, insurance, license plate accuracy, honoring paid seats, and complying with all laws and school rules.

Rider responsibilities

Riders are responsible for choosing rides carefully, arriving on time, paying required amounts, respecting drivers and other riders, and using safety tools when appropriate.

Payments and payouts

Driver-set ride prices may include LinkUp commission. Payment and payout flows must use approved payment providers before production. LinkUp may withhold or reverse payouts for fraud, disputes, cancellations, or safety issues.

Safety and liability

Rides involve real-world risk. Users agree to use the service at their own risk to the fullest extent allowed by law. LinkUp should obtain legal and insurance advice before public launch.

Account limits

LinkUp may suspend accounts, remove listings, disable chats, or restrict access to protect users, enforce these Terms, or comply with law.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LinkUp collects, uses, protects, and deletes information in this student ride-sharing prototype. Have a qualified attorney review this before launch.

Information we collect

We collect account details, university email verification details, ride and request details, approximate and exact trip coordinates, reserved seats, ride chats, driver ratings, live trip tracking locations, vehicle details, masked payment method details, and driver payout contact details.

How we use information

We use information to verify student access, match riders and drivers, reserve seats, process payments, support safety features, send account emails, show ride history, prevent abuse, and operate the service.

Sensitive information

License plates are intended to be visible only to confirmed riders. Live location is intended for active trip safety sharing. Payment cards and payout bank details should be handled by Stripe in production, not stored directly by LinkUp.

Retention

LinkUp should keep sensitive data only as long as needed for safety, payments, dispute handling, legal compliance, and app operations. Users should be able to request account or data deletion before production launch.

Security

We use password hashing, session cookies, access checks, rate limits, safer response headers, and limited storage of payment data. No system is perfectly secure, and production launch requires a real database, HTTPS, monitoring, backups, and incident response procedures.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the LinkUp operator at the support email listed by the app owner.

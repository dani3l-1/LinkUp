About LinkUp

Student rides, built around trust.

LinkUp helps college students find, offer, and share rides with people from their own school network. The goal is simple: make campus travel easier, safer, and more affordable without turning rides into a commercial taxi service.

For riders

Find rides posted by verified students, reserve seats, chat with your driver, and keep your trip details organized in one place.

For drivers

Post available seats, choose ride preferences, manage confirmed riders, and track weekly earnings through your wallet.

For schools

LinkUp is designed for university communities, with school-based access, public profiles, reporting, blocking, and shared ride stats.

Safety first

Profiles, policy agreement, trip chat, ride history, reporting tools, and optional Track My Trip links help keep users informed before and during rides.

LinkUp coordinates student ride sharing. Drivers remain independent users responsible for their vehicle, license, insurance, route, and safe driving.

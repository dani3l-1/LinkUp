New LinkUp rules

Review what changed

Review and accept the latest LinkUp policies to keep using ride services.

Service access

LinkUp ride services require an approved university account, verified email, and agreement to the latest Terms and Conditions and Privacy Notice.

Ride safety

Riders and drivers must provide accurate trip, seat, vehicle, pickup, and drop-off information. Do not use LinkUp to create unsafe, misleading, or unauthorized rides.

Payments and reservations

Seats are only reserved after checkout is completed. Driver-set prices, payout details, and commission rules must be accurate before a ride is listed or accepted.

Location sharing

Track My Trip location sharing is for rider safety. Only share tracking links with trusted people, and stop tracking when the trip is complete.

Privacy notice

LinkUp collects account, university, ride, session, usage, device, and payment summary data to operate the service, protect users, and support legal compliance.

Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: May 8, 2026. By using LinkUp, you agree to these Terms. If you do not agree, you may not use the Service.

Eligibility

You must be an enrolled student, faculty, or staff at an accredited institution, use a valid university email, be at least 18 years old, and keep your account information accurate.

Nature of the Service

LinkUp is a peer-to-peer ride coordination platform. It does not provide transportation, employ drivers, own vehicles, or operate as a taxi or common carrier.

Driver responsibilities

Drivers must hold a valid license; use a registered, insured vehicle; comply with traffic laws; avoid impaired driving; and treat passengers with respect.

Passenger responsibilities

Passengers acknowledge rides are offered by private individuals, behave safely and respectfully, and arrive at agreed pickup locations on time.

All user responsibilities

Users must not act unlawfully, post fraudulent listings, harass others, bypass security features, impersonate others, or scrape the Service.

Payments and cost-sharing

Payments are voluntary cost-sharing contributions, not commercial fares. Payout details are processed by third-party processors; LinkUp never stores full card or bank numbers.

Liability and indemnification

LinkUp provides the Service "as is." Users indemnify LinkUp for claims arising from their use of the Service, rides offered or joined, or submitted content.

Termination and disputes

LinkUp may suspend accounts at any time. Disputes go to good-faith negotiation first; if unresolved after 30 days, to binding arbitration at LinkUp's election.

Contact

Questions? Email ridewlinkup@gmail.com.

Privacy Notice

Effective Date: May 8, 2026. This notice explains how LinkUp collects, uses, and shares personal data.

Data we collect

Account info, payment/payout summaries, ride data, communications, session data, and device/browser info. Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes only.

Data we do not collect

We do not store full card numbers, CVV codes, bank account/routing numbers, GPS location, biometrics, or government-issued ID.

How we use data

Authentication, ride matching, payouts, security, fraud prevention, and service improvement. No automated profiling with legal effects without consent.

Data sharing

We don't sell personal data. Limited sharing with verified users, payment processors, infrastructure providers, and legal authorities where required.

Security

bcrypt password hashing, secure session cookies, HTTPS, rate limiting, and hardened response headers.

Cookies

Only strictly necessary session cookies. No ad cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics.

Contact

Privacy questions: ridewlinkup@gmail.com.

About LinkUp

Student rides, built around trust.

LinkUp connects university students to share rides within their school network — safer, simpler, and more affordable than going alone.

For riders

Browse rides from verified students, reserve a seat, and chat with your driver — all in one place.

For drivers

Post seats, manage confirmed riders, and track earnings through your wallet.

For schools

School-based access, public profiles, reporting, and shared ride stats — built for university communities.

Safety

Profiles, ride history, reporting tools, moderation review, and optional Track My Trip links help students stay informed.

Community rules
  • Post only real rides and requests you intend to honor.
  • No harassment, threats, discrimination, scams, impersonation, or pressure to move off app.
  • Report unsafe behavior, fake listings, payment issues, or serious ride problems from the ride tools.
  • LinkUp may suspend accounts, remove rides or requests, and document moderation decisions.
LinkUp coordinates ride sharing. Drivers are independent users responsible for their vehicle, license, insurance, and safe driving. For support, email ridewlinkup@gmail.com.

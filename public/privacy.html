Privacy Notice

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Notice explains how LinkUp ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal data when you use the LinkUp application and related services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the UK GDPR / EU GDPR and, where applicable, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Please read this Notice carefully. By using the App, you acknowledge you have read and understood how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Account information: name, university email address, password stored as a cryptographic hash — never in plain text.
  • Profile information: payout method/contact details saved by drivers; masked payment card details saved by passengers.
  • Ride data: pickup and drop-off location coordinates, travel date and time, available seats, and any notes attached to a listing.
  • Communications: messages or support requests you send to us.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Session data: authentication session tokens and cookies necessary to keep you logged in.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, timestamps, and IP address used for security and rate-limiting purposes.
  • Device and browser information: browser type, operating system, and similar technical identifiers.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store full payment card numbers, CVV codes, bank account numbers, or routing numbers.
  • We do not collect real-time GPS location from your device. Location data is entered manually by users when posting a ride.
  • We do not collect biometric data, government-issued ID, or sensitive personal data as defined under GDPR Article 9.

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes and lawful bases:

  • Account creation and authentication — necessary to perform the contract with you.
  • Matching riders with available rides — necessary to provide the core service.
  • Facilitating driver payouts — necessary to perform the contract with drivers.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and rate-limiting — based on our legitimate interests in protecting the platform and its users.
  • Improving the Service — based on our legitimate interests in improving functionality and user experience, using aggregated, de-identified data where possible.
  • Legal compliance — where required by applicable law.

We will not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects without your explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: your display name and ride details, including pickup/drop-off area, date, seats, and notes, are visible to other verified university users on the platform so that rides can be coordinated.
  • With payment processors: payout details and masked card information are shared with our third-party payment processor(s) solely to process transactions. These processors are contractually bound to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable law.
  • With service providers: we may share data with hosting providers, security services, and analytics tools that help us operate the App, under strict data processing agreements.
  • For legal reasons: we may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • In a business transfer: if LinkUp is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We may retain certain data for longer periods where required by law or for legitimate security purposes, such as logs relevant to fraud investigations.

Ride history data is retained for a reasonable period to allow dispute resolution and payout processing, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Passwords stored using bcrypt or equivalent cryptographic hashing.
  • Session cookies configured with HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite=Strict flags.
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Rate limiting and request-size restrictions to mitigate abuse.
  • Secure response headers to reduce common web vulnerabilities.

However, no system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach that poses a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

7. Cookies and Tracking

LinkUp uses strictly necessary session cookies to maintain your authenticated session. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so will prevent you from remaining logged in.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: to receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): to request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction: to restrict how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: to object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • CCPA rights for California residents: the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: ridewlinkup@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days or within the timeframe required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. International Transfers

If you are located in the UK or European Economic Area (EEA), your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK/EEA. Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, in accordance with applicable data protection law.

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice in the App or by email. The date at the top of this document indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated Notice.

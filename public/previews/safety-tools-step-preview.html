Step 7: Use safety tools during the ride

Safety tools are available to riders and drivers during every active trip.

TrackShare live location

Send a trip link to someone you trust. The link expires when sharing stops.

RecordSafety recording

Start audio recording if something feels wrong. Stored for 30 days for reports.

VerifyArrival code

Give or collect the 6-digit code so each stop is confirmed.

CheckDoor safety

Riders confirm the door opens from inside and child lock is off.

ReportContact LinkUp

Report unsafe behavior directly to LinkUp support from the ride flow.

TrustVerified students

Profiles, ratings, and .edu verification help students choose safer rides.

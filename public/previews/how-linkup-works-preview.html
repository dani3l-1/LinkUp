Step 1

Sign up and join your university network

Create an account with your university email. If your campus is not active yet, LinkUp saves your account and places you on the waitlist.

  • 1Create your account. Use your .edu email so LinkUp can keep the network student-only.
  • 2Keep your member number. Your waitlist page confirms your account and shows campus demand.
  • 3Edit your profile. Add details before your campus opens so you are ready to ride or drive.
Step 2

Find a ride and review it before booking

Browse available seats from verified drivers and inspect the ride before you reserve.

  • 1Open Find a ride. Search listings by route, date, and time.
  • 2Review the car. Check driver details, vehicle info, seat availability, price, rules, and booked passengers.
  • 3Reserve your seat. Pick the seat you want and complete checkout.
Step 3

Request a ride when no listing fits

If you cannot find the right ride, post what you need so drivers can respond.

  • 1Set your route. Enter pickup, drop-off, date, and time.
  • 2Add ride details. Include rider count, price willing to pay, preferences, and notes.
  • 3Post the request. Verified drivers can see it and offer a matching ride.
Step 4

List a ride if you are driving

Drivers can post empty seats for a trip they are already taking.

  • 1Add the trip. Enter route, departure time, vehicle details, and seat inventory.
  • 2Set pricing. Add seat price and optional parking or airport fee.
  • 3Publish rules. Set no smoking, same school, pickup radius, and other rider-facing details.
Step 5

Use the ride dashboard before pickup

After booking, your dashboard gives you the next steps for a safer ride.

  • 1Check your ride details. Confirm driver, vehicle, seat, time, and arrival code.
  • 2Follow the checklist. Verify the driver, check door safety, and optionally share live tracking.
  • 3Finish the ride. Give the arrival code and rate the driver after the trip.
Step 6

Use driver tools during the trip

Drivers get a route, passenger manifest, checklist, and map links in one place.

  • 1Review riders. Check names, seats, and drop-off locations before leaving.
  • 2Open the route. Launch Google Maps or Apple Maps with the stops already entered.
  • 3Collect codes. Ask each rider for their arrival code at drop-off.
Step 7

Use safety tools when needed

Safety tools are available to riders and drivers during every active trip.

  • 1Share tracking. Send your live trip to someone you trust.
  • 2Record if needed. Start safety recording if something feels wrong.
  • 3Report issues. Contact LinkUp support after unsafe behavior or policy violations.
